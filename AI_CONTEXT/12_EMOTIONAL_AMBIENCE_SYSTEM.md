
# Emotional Ambience System

Purpose:
Add atmospheric emotional feedback to the interface.

Layers:
- gradient background
- map overlay
- marker reactions
- popup ambience

Animations may use GSAP and its plugins to enhance interaction.

Allowed GSAP plugins include:
ScrollTrigger
Flip
MotionPathPlugin
MorphSVGPlugin
DrawSVGPlugin
SplitText
Observer
Draggable

GSAP controls motion only.
Theming must remain controlled by SYX tokens.
